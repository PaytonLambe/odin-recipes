Zuppa

Desctiption

Zuppa Toscana is a favorite dish among my family. This hearty soup is traditionally full of vegetables such as kale, potatoes, and zucchini. It also inludes savory meats such as italian sausage and bacon.

This delicious wintery recipe will be made paleo so there will be a few necessary substitutes. I have also made some additional changes for my own preference. I like to add one or two red bell peppers rather than the bacon to my soup.

    Ingredients
  • 1lb Italian Sausage
  • Crushed red pepper to taste
  • 1 yellow onion diced
  • 5 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 large Russet potatoes
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 5 cups Kale
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 red bell peppers
    Steps
  1. Cook italian sausage and diced onion together in large stock pot. Once browned, drain
  2. Return meat and onion to pot and add red pepper flakes, potatoes, garlic, salt, and pepper. Cook for one minute or until you really begin to smell the garlic, then add the broth.
  3. Let simmer for 20 minutes then add red bell pepper. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  4. Add coconut milk and kale. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
