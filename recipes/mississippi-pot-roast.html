Mississippi Pot Roast

Desctiption

This roast is extremely easy and is always a go-to when I catch the chuck on sale at the store. It involves few ingredients and can be made in a crock pot or dutch oven.

Although not included in the recipe, my preference is eat the roast over mashed potatoes with a side of roasted carrots and a dinner role. Yet another winter favorite.

    Ingredients
  • 3 lb chuck roast
  • 1 packet of dry onion soup mix
  • 8 peperonchini peppers
  • 1 packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1 tsp salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup butter
    Steps
  1. Salt and pepper the meat. Heat skillet on high heat. Add olive oil and sear the meat on all sides. Once seared, move meat to crock pot.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients to the crock pot. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours.
  3. Enjoy!
