Italian Red Sauce

Description

This recipe originally came from a lasagna recipe. It has since been slightly modified and applied all sorts of Italian dished. It can be made vegetarian but I personally enjoy it with an even mix of hot italian sausage and ground beef.

This meal can be enjoyed year round but I love the winter for meals like this. If you have read any of my other recipes, you will already know that cold weather cooking is one of my favorite things.

    Ingredients
  • 1 lb hot italian sausage
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 minced onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 12 oz can tomato paste
  • 15 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1-2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp fresh chopped parsley
  1. Cook minced onion, sausage, and beef in large pot. Once browned, drain and return to pot.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook while stirring for one minute or until garlic becomes fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water to pot. Mix ingredients together.
  4. Add sugar, basil, fennel seeds, salt, pepper, italian seasoning, and parsley. Stir and bring to a simmer.
  5. Cover pot with lid and let simmer at a low heat for at least an hour and a half. Stir occasionally.
  6. Add to any of your favorite pasta dishes!
